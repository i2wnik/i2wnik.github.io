Borovoe

The pearl of Kazakhstan

Rock Zhumbaktas

In the northern part of Lake Borovoe (Auliekol), between the spurs of Kokshe and Temirtau, a bay juts deep into the shore. In the middle of the bay there is a stone island, a pile of weathered mattress-like granite layers. This rock - Zhumbaktas (Stone - a mystery) resembles either a mysterious Sphinx with frozen impassive features, or a pensive young man with a shock of hair developing in the wind.It all depends on the fantasy and imagination of the person. If we go from the cordon to the Ablai Khan glade, then first from the cordon, if we climb higher, then we will see from above the profile of cartoons - from whom Cheburashka, further from the boat station - the profile of a beautiful young girl, slowly walk along and the profile of a young girl turns into the profile of an old woman , and if you swim up to it from the side of the Okzhetpes sanatorium, you can see the face of a mysterious beast (Sphinx). One of the legends about Okzhetpes says that this is a captured Kalmyk woman who fell from the top of Okzhetpes.

Mountain Bolektau

Mount Bolektau is a natural observation deck of the Burabay resort. From 147 meters in height, breathtaking views of the Blue Bay, Zhumbaktas rock, Okzhetpes mountain and Borovoe and Bolshoye Chebachye lakes open up.Nature has surprisingly created a real observation deck, as if specially attracting people to enjoy all the beauty of the region. It offers a view of the Borovoe and Bolshoye Chebachye lakes, the Zhumbaktas rock, the Okzhetpes mountain and the Blue Bay.

Kenesary Cave

Kenesary Khan was born on this land and loved to hunt, so he often spent the night among the forest and mountains in a small cave. From the Abylai Khan glade or Okzhetpes mountain, you can walk independently along the forest path with stone and wooden steps, following the paved route.The cave itself resembles an eight-rope yurt, and on top there is a round opening, similar to a shanyrak, which served as a chimney. On the same northern coast you will find the Grove of dancing birches and mountain waterfalls. If you are relaxing on one of the other lakes or you want to plunge headlong into the atmosphere of Borovoye and learn in detail ancient legends, then it is better to use the services of guides.

Museum of Nature

In the Museum of Nature Burabay National Park, you will meet with representatives of the flora and fauna of the protected area, many of which are included in the Red Book. It is located at 38 Kenesary village Borovoye, it works daily from 10.00 am to 18.30 pm, except Mondays. Cost - 600 tenge.Your attention will be presented to representatives of the fauna: mammals, carnivores, birds and insects. The first room presents the stuffed animals in the region and a collection of insects.
