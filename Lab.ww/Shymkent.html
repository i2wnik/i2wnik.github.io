Shymkent

Baydibek bi monument

The highest monument in the country dedicated to Baidibek bi is located in the northern part of the city of Shymkent. Baidibek Karashauly played an important role in uniting the Kazakh people and today is known as a significant historical personality of Kazakhstan. The monument is located on a hill equal to 220 meters, and the height of the figure itself is 10 meters. But taking into account the embankments, the monument to the famous biy belongs to the highest in the world.

Following historical data, Baidibek bi was a very honest and reasonable person who could resolve any dispute. Therefore, the sculpture of the great judge is depicted as a figure that stretches out its arms to the sky, personifying the open character of the ancient judge and the breadth of his soul. The Baidibek bi monument is one of the main attractions of the city, which has become a valuable architectural monument and embodied the idea of ​​the unity of the people of Kazakhstan.

Arboretum

There is an incredibly large and beautiful arboretum in Shymkent! A favorite place for relaxation, picnics in the fresh air, now fashionable photo shoots and walks of lovers.

At the moment, the park has separate bike and horse paths, fountains, a water cascade that turns into the “Kingdom of Love” waterfall. Since then, newlyweds and lovers fell in love with the arboretum. New products in the arboretum include fountains, a 142-meter water cascade, which turns into the “Kingdom of Love” waterfall. And then a new tradition arose - to make wishes under a waterfall. New settlers also appeared - squirrels, roe deer, sika deer, peacocks. Silver carp fry, geese, ducks, and swans were released into the lake. After the reconstruction, the arboretum of the city of Shymkent was added to the list of national parks of Kazakhstan, which gave it the status of a specially protected area.

Eco Village Kaskasu

In the South Kazakhstan region, Kaskasu is nestled - a charming corner of wild nature, 50 km east of Shymkent. These are quite high-mountainous places, raised above sea level by almost 2000 meters by the spurs of the Tien Shan. The recreational qualities of the local water and air have been known for a long time, but a full-fledged tourism industry has begun to develop recently.

Today in Kaskasu there are several sanatoriums and recreation areas, among them the largest one is “Altyn Dan”. It is a cottage town built right in the middle of two steep slopes that form a valley. Sanatoriums in Kaskasu work not only in summer, but also in winter, when conditions for skiing are present.
